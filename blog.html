My Birthday

Posted on February 24th 2020,22:00

15 years into my life! Kind of sounds fascinating to me. Yes, it was my Birthday today. To be Honest, Best Birthday Ever.

To start, it was a regular day and I ain't that excited for birthdays but 15 years sounded big.I got up early to mark exact 15 years of my existence, Did I tell you I was born at 5:45? And just so I reasiled my change in past 15 years or atleast what I could remember till then.In past few years I changed as a physique and as a person; more curious, enthusiastic and ,ofc. more motivated.I spent a fair amount of time thinking about my life and some deep stuff.

Overall, I had a great day(and night too).My tounge is soo sweet even right now, I've had Cakes and Doughnuts and Smoothies; I cant even count how much!!We had a grand party arranged by my Classmates and Friends it was *awesome*.At home, we had a huge dinner with fam.Interaction with a lot of people, if you ask me; never had this much attention (even though i love my privacy and solitude more).

For a person who is not a fan of Birthdays, well , this birthday was a blast for me.I had a lot of time alone , which I *absolutely* loved, but aside from that my best friends and my family made it even better!

"Couldn't have asked for a better Birthday!"


Voice Unheard

Posted on July 18th 2020,22:00

I always had this idea in the back of my mind; that I would be able to share my idea to the world. I would be heard and be noticed. Well, this could obviously be a result of the fact that I am neglected a lot, but that's not a point. the point is that I let my voice out, out there in the real world.

So...I decided to start my new Blog (on Blogspot). And it's named Voice Unheard dedicated to mine that's yearning to be heard. I've not maintained it properly yet. But soon, I think I will get a hold of it. *wink*

There must be something that you fear, everyone has something. Mine is being invisible, going unheard . This blog is totally dedicated to me finding my lost voice, accepting my thoughts as my own and not throwing them away.
